Peach and Tomato Caprese Salad

This peach caprese salad with heirloom tomatoes and fresh mozzarella is topped with a simple balsamic vinaigrette for a fantastic summer appetizer.
    Ingredients
    1. 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    2. 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
    3. 1 teaspoon flaked salt, divided
    4. 2 large heirloom tomatoes, thinly sliced
    5. 2 ripe peaches - halved, pitted, and sliced into half moons
    6. 1 (8 ounce) ball fresh mozzarella, thinly sliced
    7. 6 leaves fresh basil
    Directions
    Step1
    Whisk olive oil, balsamic vinegar,
    and 1 pinch flaked salt together in a bowl until dressing is smooth.
    Step2
    Alternate tomato slices, peach slices, mozzarella slices, and basil leaves in layers on a platter.
    Drizzle dressing over salad and sprinkle remaining flaked salt on top.
