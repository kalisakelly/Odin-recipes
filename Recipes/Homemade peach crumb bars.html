Homemade peach crumb bars

Homemade Peach Crumb Bars are a delightful dessert made with fresh peaches and a buttery crumb topping.
They are easy to make and perfect for summer when peaches are in season.
Here's a simple recipe to help you make these delicious bars:
    Ingredients
  • cooking spray
    • Crumble and Crust:
    1. 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    2. ½ teaspoon baking powderr
    3. 1 pinch salt
    4. 10 tablespoons cold unsalted butter, cut into cubes
    5. 1 large egg yolk
    6. 1 tablespoon cold water
    Peach Filling:
    1. 2 cups fresh peach slices, or more to taste
    2. ½ cup white sugar
    3. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    4. 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    5. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    6. ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
    7. ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
